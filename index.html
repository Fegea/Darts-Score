Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Darts Score mobile application created and operated by Florian EGEA. It explains what information (if any) the app collects, how that information is used, and your choices.

Summary (short version)

  • The app does not collect personally identifiable information (PII).
  • All game data (scores, match history) is stored locally on your device only.
  • The app displays ads served by Google AdMob; AdMob may collect anonymous device data.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Darts Score does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or precise geographic location. You do not need to create an account to use the app.

2. Local Data (on-device database)

The app stores match and score history in a local database on your device (for example, to let you review past games or resume matches). This data remains on your device and is not automatically uploaded to any server or shared with third parties by the app.

If you enable any backup or export feature (none enabled by default), that action will be explicitly requested by the app and described to you before any data leaves the device.

3. Permissions Used

The app declares the following permissions in its Android manifest:

  • android.permission.INTERNET — required to load and display advertisements and for any optional online features.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID — declared to use the Advertising ID required by Google Play Services for AdMob.

No location, contacts, or storage permissions are requested by default. If you see additional permission requests, the app will request them at runtime and explain why.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Ads in this app are served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous device information to deliver and personalize ads, including:

  • a resettable advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
  • approximate location information inferred from IP (not precise GPS)
  • device and app information (model, OS version, app interactions)

This information is collected by AdMob (a third party) and not by the Darts Score app itself. Ad personalization settings and opt-out options are provided by Google. For details, see Google’s privacy resources: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/.

If you prefer non-personalized ads (NPA), configure the ad personalization settings on your device or follow Google’s instructions to control ad personalization.

5. How We Use Information

The app itself does not transmit or process personal information. Any anonymous device data collected by third-party ad providers is used for ad serving, analytics and improving ad relevance. The local match history is used only to display your previous games within the app.

6. Data Retention

Local game data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it (for example, via a built-in delete or clear history action) or you uninstall the app. Third-party advertisers retain the anonymous data they collect according to their own policies.

7. Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect local data on the device. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure — the app cannot guarantee absolute security of data on your device.

8. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

9. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services (Google AdMob) that have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies to understand how they use data.

10. Your Rights

Because the app does not collect personal information, there is little personal data for us to modify or delete. If you have concerns about local game data, you can:

  • delete match history from within the app (if the app exposes such a function),
  • uninstall the app to remove app data stored in the app's private storage on the device,
  • contact us at the address below for any specific requests or clarifications.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time (for example, to reflect changes in third-party services or legal requirements). The "Last updated" line below will show the month and year of the latest update.
