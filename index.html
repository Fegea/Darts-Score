Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Darts Score application, created and operated by Florian EGEA. Its purpose is to inform users about the collection, use, and disclosure of information when using the application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Postal address
  • Precise geographic location

The application does not require you to create an account and does not store personal profiles of any kind.

2. Automatically Collected Data

The app does not directly collect or store user data. However, certain third-party services integrated into the app (such as advertising providers) may collect anonymous device data for their own purposes.

3. Permissions Used

The app uses the following Android permissions:

android.permission.INTERNET
Used to access the internet for displaying advertisements and improving the user experience.

com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID
Required to access the advertising ID used by Google Play Services.

4. Advertising (AdMob)

The application uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect anonymous data to personalize and improve ad relevance.

AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (resettable by the user)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • App interaction information (e.g., views of non-personalized ads)

AdMob does not collect information that personally identifies you.

To learn more about how Google uses data and your options to control ad personalization, please review Google's Privacy Policy:
https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/
